# Project: Rate My Set

## Description
Concept for an entertainment-industry forum where cast and crew can post honest reviews of their on-set experiences without fear of retaliation from productions, casting networks, or showrunners. Early artifacts: a workflow sitemap (Home → Results → Reviews/Lists, Accounts → HQ → Rate-a-set → Survey, with a moderator step on new-show submissions) and a questionnaire structured into Professionalism, Food, Safety, and Green Production sections with red/yellow/green scoring, plus a scorecard that surfaces an Overall score, category scores, and a count of harassment/discrimination reports. Starting point for brainstorming: scope, trust/anonymity model, moderation, and legal exposure.
